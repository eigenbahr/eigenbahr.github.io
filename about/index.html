About Me

I’m a software engineer at the National Snow and Ice Data Center (NSIDC). NSIDC is part of the Cooperative Institute for Research in Environmental Sciences at the University of Colorado Boulder. I develop software for scientific data products, data analysis, and visualization. I work mainly in Python, JavaScript / TypeScript, and develop both on-prem and cloud (AWS) deployed systems.
